About the Department

Philosophy department is the most prestigious department of the college. The department was established in 1984 and founder faculty is Dr. S. P. Pandey. In this subject P.G. course started in 2018. Value added course has been running since 2015. A Philosophy Council has been formed in the philosophy department. All the faculty members are actively contributing as members of different academic and administrative committee of the College.

  • Master of Arts (M.A.)
  • Ph.D.

  • Value Added Course in Museum & Heritage Studies
