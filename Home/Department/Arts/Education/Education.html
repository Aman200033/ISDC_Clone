About the Department

The Department of Education was established in the year 1970 and has been actively imparting quality education since then. There are four sanctioned posts in the department, which are all filled up. The department comprises four faculty members, two are Associate Professors with M.Phil and Ph.D. degrees and two are Assistant Professors with Ph.D. degrees. All the faculty members are actively contributing to the academic and administrative activities of the college. Dr. Man Singh is the convener of the department, who is also the Proctor of the college and Assistant Co-ordinator of UPRTOU, Allahabad. He has several research papers and a published book to his credit.Dr. Anuja Saluja is the senior faculty of the department. She is also the Convener of the Women Cell and Department of the English. She has a number of research projects, articles and a book to her credit. She has also organized many personality development and capacity building workshops in the college. The other faculty members are Dr. Shailesh Kumar Yadav and Dr. Avinash Pandey who are actively and efficiently contributing to the teaching-learning activities of the department.
