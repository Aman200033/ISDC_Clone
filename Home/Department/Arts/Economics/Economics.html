About the Department

The Department of Economics came into existence in 1970 along with the establishment of the college. Its Founder head was Dr. J. S. L. Srivastava, an eminent scholar and astute administrator who became Principal of the College and remained in the seat till retirement. Dr. A. P. Srivastava and Dr. R. K. Markande were in the department. Dr. Markande retired in 2018. Presently the department has four faculty members, Dr. A.K. Srivastav,Dr. Harshmani Singh, Dr. Ved Prakash Mishra and Ms. Garima Maurya.All the faculty members have specialization in different disciplines of economics such as Econometrics, Development Economics, Public Economics, Economic Policy and International Economics.All the faculty members are actively contributing as members of various academic and administrative committees of the college.
