About the Department

The Department of Medieval and Modern History was established in the year 1982. Since then, it is actively contributing in enhancing and strengthening the academic excellence as well as the administrative environment of the college. At present the Department is enriched with highly qualified, efficient and experienced faculty members. Dr. Gayatri Singh (Associate Professor), Dr. Dheeraj Kumar Chaudhary(Associate Professor) , Dr. Rachana Singh (Associate Professor). Dr. Narendra Kumar Singh and Dr. Refaq Ahmad have recently joined the department as Assistant Professors. All the faculty members are extensively contributing to the academic and administrative growth of the college.The founder member of the department Dr. Gayatri Singh is rendering her valuable services since its inception and has completed almost 37 years in service. She has several research papers and a published book to her credit.Senior faculty member Dr. Dheeraj Kumar Chaudhary is In charge of the executive committee of the Faculty Development Centre, Convener of the Documentation Cell and President of the teachers union of the college. He has received Best Teacher Award from the Vice Chancellor University of Allahabad for his outstanding services.
