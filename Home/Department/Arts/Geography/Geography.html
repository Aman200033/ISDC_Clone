About the Department

The Department of Geography came into existence in 2018 (session 2018-2019) under the Self - Finance scheme. The department was looked after previously by Dr. Roohi Rawat and Dr. Parvej Alam and Dr. Rekha Yadav is now looking the department.The Department is enriched with highly qualified, efficient and experienced faculty members. In recent session, Department has three batches of Geography students. First year batch strength is 108 and 2nd year batch strength is 96 while the third year batch has 42 students.
