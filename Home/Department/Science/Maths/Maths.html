About the Department

•Department of mathematics was established in 1994 as the compulsory subject of B. Sc. (Mathematics) group. Since session 2018-19, mathematics is also allowed as one of the subject in B. A.. The department organized mathematics aptitude test for all the students of the college. The mathematics department help there student for mathematics scholarship exam. •The founder faculty members were Anil Kumar Garg and Awadhesh Singh. Current faculty members are Abhishek Singh, Varun Raj Rawat, Vishwajeet Tiwari.
