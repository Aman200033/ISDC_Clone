About the Department

The Department of Chemistry was established in the year 2001 (session 2001-2002) under the Self finance scheme by Dr. Sanjeev Kumar and Dr. H. S. Verma. A big Laboratory having capacity approx. 90 students once in a time was developed by the founder members for the practical. Chemistry is a combination with the Biology group as well as with Maths group. It is well known that the Chemistry is the most important subject due to valuable applications in the field of Pharmaceutical science, Agriculture, rubber industry, polymer industry, paint industry, colour industry, Defence and explosive and in our daily life. The department is enriched with highly qualified, efficient and experienced faculty members. Dr. Sanjeev Kumar and Dr. Rajesh Verma are extensively contributing to the academic and administrative growth of the college.
