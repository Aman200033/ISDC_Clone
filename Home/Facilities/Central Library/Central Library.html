About Library

The college has a separate newly built Library building having nearly fifty thousand books and regular subscription of ninety three international and national journals; twenty one magazines and daily newspapers. It caters the needs of more than 5000 students at undergraduate and postgraduate level. The Library services are fully automated. The Library has reading halls at the ground floor. One Reading hall is for UG students and other for PG & Research students. The reading halls can accommodate two hundred fifty students. Computer Lab of 50latest systems is fully functional to access the e-resources.The Library uses the SOUL 2.0 - Library Management Software.The software is used for Cataloguing, Classification, Circulation etc. The Library is well equipped with all the modern facilities and resources (print and electronic) in the forms of CDROMs, DVD-ROM, books, journals, etc.The library remains open on all working days from 9AM to 5 PM. To become a member, students should fill and submit a prescribed membership form to the library.The Library has a total collection of around 50000 which includes books, serials, non-book materials etc. The Library subscribes to 93 national and international journals, 10 Magazine and 11 Newspapers. Library also has collections by way of donation.

membership

All the bonafide students of Iswar Saran College, teaching, non-teaching staff members have right to access the Library. To become a member of library, bonafide students should fill a form available with the Library Office. The registered members are issued Library Card corresponding to their entitlement.

Membership Rules

1. The bonafide students and staff members have right to access the library and use it for reading and studying during working hours and days.
2. The membership of the library can be obtained by the bonafide student and staff members of the College by filling a prescribed form with the College Library Office for issuing booksand availing different facilities of the library.A membership card is issued by the Library Office. The member should produce the membership card while getting books, etc. issued in her/her name or availing different facilities.
3. Students and Staff member when leaving College permanently must deposit the cards with the Library Office and get their account cleared.
4. In case membership card is lost or misplaced the library office should be informed in writing as early as possible. Members can obtain a new card in place of lost or misplaced card. In case of loss or damage of card Rs.50 shall be charged from the member.

Rules for Issue, Return and Maintenance of Books

1. The members can issue books in their name against the membership card as per their entitlement. While issuing the books, the user must check the books that the same is in order. In case of any deformity is noticed by the user the same should be reported to the counter assistant. Books are issued for a limited period; hence, the user should note the date by which she/he is required to return the book.
2. The issued books must be returned to the Library on or before the date of return. Returning book late shall attract fine.
3. In case the book is lost/misplaced by the member he/she has to replace the same with a new one or shall be required to pay the cost of book at current price.
4. Students in possession of any library book must return same before the end of classes (official date of ending classes) in each semester failure of which appropriate fine shall be levied.
5. The user must maintain and handle the books, etc. issued to her/him properly, carefully and safely. She/he should not mark on it, underline on the texts, write anything on it, and deface it in any form.
6. The Librarian, in the interest of Library and Institution, has the discretion to recall any book issued to the user at any time.

Discipline and Decorum in the Library

1. The user/members shall maintain perfect silence in the library reading halls. Talking loudly, gossiping, chatting, etc. is strictly prohibited.
2. Running, pushing passing gestures and teasing is strictly prohibited.
3. Eatables and soft drinks are not allowed in the library.
4. Use of mobile phone is prohibited in the library. Mobile phones should be kept under silent mode in the library.
5. Marking, underlining, doodling or defacing any Books, Newspapers, Magazines, etc. of the library prohibited.
6. Carrying any book, published material, or any property of the library outside the library without proper issue or authorization shall be deemed as gross indiscipline.
7. Defacing, damaging and mishandling the property of the library shall be deemed as gross indiscipline.Non-compliance or breaching the rules of Discipline and decorum in the library shall attract disciplinary action and fine or cancellation of membership or both.

e-Resource Centre

Library has setup a separate & dedicated internet service area for the students. More than 50 PCs have been installed for students to access internet and online resources. Apart from e- Journals, college has institutional membership of N-List and DELNET.
